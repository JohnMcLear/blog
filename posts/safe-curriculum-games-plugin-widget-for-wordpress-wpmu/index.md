**

Description:

**

  

Put the top 5 curriculum games from [Primary Games Arena](http://primarygamesarena.com/) onto your blog.

  

**Installation:**

1. Download from here
2. Unzip to wp-contents/plugins
3. Activate site wide (In plugins)
4. Log into wp-admin
5. Click Appearance > Widgets
6. Drag the widget onto your sidebar

Example:

  

<iframe frameborder="0" src="http://primarygamesarena.com/top5.php?font=black&amp;bg=white" width="210" height="645"></iframe>

Step 1. Log into your blog Step 2. Click Appearance-> Widgets Step 3. Drag RSS over to your sidebar. Step 4. Paste **_http://newsrss.bbc.co.uk/rss/cbbc\_news/homepage/rss.xml_** in where it asks for RSS feed URL Step 5. Give the widget a title. \*optional

Note: [PrimaryBlogger](http://primaryblogger.co.uk) users can just drag the CBBC Widget onto their sidebar.

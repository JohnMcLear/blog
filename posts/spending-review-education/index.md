Today the UK government let us know how they are going to spend on Edu.  This is what I got from it..

1. Budget raise from £35bn to £39bn
2. Schools will have more autonomy
3. £2.5bn pupil premium - supports disadvantaged kids, includes 15 hourr per week entitlement
4. Surestart is protected
5. 15 free hours for disadvantaged 2 year olds
6. DFE lost 1% buget
7. Central admin lost 1/3 budget
8. Phase out BSF

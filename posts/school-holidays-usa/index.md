School holidays in the USA are assigned at a district level so vary more than any other country. There are currently 16500+ different available districts so it is important you [check your own district or school calendar before booking a holiday or vacation](http://us.myschoolholidays.com).

## When are the school holidays?

### Spring Break

Spring break tends to fall around Easter and usually lasts a week. The date of Easter Varies so [see the USA public holiday dates as a reference point](http://us.myschoolholidays.com/bank-holidays).

### Winter Break

Winter Break surrounds Christmas and New Year, the Holiday usually lasts a week or two. Winter is on the 25th of December and New Year is on the 1st of January. [You should check your school or district calendar to see when the holidays begin and end for your school.](http://us.myschoolholidays.com)

### Thanksgiving Break

Thanksgiving break surrounds the fourth Thursday of November, schools receive a minimum of two days on this holiday however some schools extend this break up to a week. The date of Thanksgiving Varies so [see the USA public holiday dates as a reference point](http://us.myschoolholidays.com/bank-holidays).

### Summer Break

The Summer break tends to begin late June and runs through till mid August. This Break represents the end of the school year and the beginning of a new school year.

## How the Holiday dates assigned

Each district is a member of a region, for example [these are the districts in the region of Alabama](http://us.myschoolholidays.com/atoz.php?region=Alabama). Because each district has unique holiday dates and often each school assigns unique teacher training and snow days you should [check your holiday dates for your school at us.MySchoolHolidays.com](http://us.myschoolholidays.com)

The first day of school tends to fall in late August/early September and the school year tends to end between in late June.

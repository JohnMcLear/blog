**Register for Google Apps Education Account**

1. Visit: [http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/edu/get\_apps.html](http://www.google.com/a/help/intl/en/edu/get_apps.html)
2. Click KS12
3. Click Sign Up Now
4. Enter your domain name, click Continue sign up
5. Fill in the next form and click Continue. Continue filling in forms until you get to your control panel.
6. Wait up to a week while Google enable Google Apps Education Edition.

**Do not forget your username and password!**

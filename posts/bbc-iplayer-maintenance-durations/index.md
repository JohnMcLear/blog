The BBC do period maintenance on the iPlayer service which requires the a temporary shutdown.

  

The maintenance duration is usually between 5-60 minutes, maintenance is usually scheduled to take place outside of peek viewing hours, beginning at 10pm and continuing into the night.

  

In the mean time you should check out [Ted talks](http://www.ted.com/talks).
